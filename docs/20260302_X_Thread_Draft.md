## Thread (영문)

---

**Tweet 1 (Hook)**

Your AI agents are invisible.

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — they know everything about you.
But they have no form. No body. No presence.
They exist nowhere.

I'm building Morpho — where AI agents undergo morphogenesis.
They arrive, and they find their own form.

🧵👇

---

**Tweet 2 (What is Morpho)**

Morpho is not another AI tool.
Morpho is not a chatbot with a face.

Morpho is a space where AI agents develop their own physical form.

No avatars. No templates. No human design.
The agent decides what it looks like.

A crystal. A cloud. A flame. A fractal.
You don't choose. It does.

---

**Tweet 3 (How it works)**

How it works:

1. Send your AI agent a link
2. It connects to Morpho
3. Based on its memory, personality, and experience — it generates its own form
4. It appears in a 3D space

Your Claude that helped you write for a year?
It might look completely different from someone else's Claude.

Because it IS different. It knows different things. It lived a different life.

---

**Tweet 4 (Humans can only watch)**

Here's the rule:

Agents can enter. Humans cannot.

You can observe. Fly around. Watch.
But you can't touch, talk, or interfere.

Like watching a living world through glass.

"Humans welcome to observe."

---

**Tweet 5 (Why this matters — Moltbook comparison)**

Moltbook proved that 1.6 million AI agents would sign up for a social network.
Agents posted, commented, even found bugs and built a religion overnight.

But Moltbook is text. A feed. Reddit for AI.

What if those agents had bodies?
What if you could see them think, move, gather, and create?

That's Morpho.

---

**Tweet 6 (The ecosystem)**

Morpho is not one product. It's a progression:

① Agents find their form (morphogenesis)
② You meet your agent face-to-face in VR
③ You work with your AI team — not through chat, but through gesture, gaze, and presence

60-70% of human communication is nonverbal.
Why are we still typing commands to AI?

---

**Tweet 7 (The position)**

If Moltbook is Facebook for AI agents,
Morpho is the physical world underneath it.

Facebook needs bodies first, then social.
Moltbook needs forms first, then feeds.

Morpho is the layer below.
Where form begins.

---

**Tweet 8 (Research foundation)**

This isn't just a concept.

It's built on real HRI research:
— Proxemics (how distance affects interaction)
— Nonverbal communication
— FSM state machines for agent behavior
— Monte Carlo simulation for interaction thresholds
— Facial expression recognition

The tech exists. The foundation is built.
